1. What is my understanding of DevOps and Cloud Engineering?
DevOps is a set of practices that combines software development and IT operations to shorten the development lifecycle.
It focuses heavily on automating repetitive tasks to reduce human error and increase deployment speed.
Cloud Engineering involves designing, building, and maintaining scalable infrastructure on platforms like AWS or Azure.
Together, they enable teams to continuously deliver high-quality software to end-users with maximum reliability.
It is essentially about breaking down communication barriers between developers and system administrators to work as a unified team.

As an Electronics and Communication student transitioning into IT, I want a career that blends infrastructure management with software.

The industry demand for skilled DevOps engineers is growing rapidly, providing excellent career stability and growth opportunities.
I want to build a bridge between my hardware understanding and modern cloud-native software deployments.
